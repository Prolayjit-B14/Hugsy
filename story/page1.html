Leo's Big Adventure

Leo is excited! Today, his class is going to the zoo. He grabs his backpack and heads for the door.

What should Leo do?

Leo checks his bag

He finds that he has forgotten his snacks and water.

Leo runs to the bus

Uh-oh! He forgot his water and snack.

Leo asks for help

The teacher gives him a water bottle and an apple. Now he can enjoy the trip!

Leo stays quiet

As the day goes on, Leo feels thirsty and tired. His teacher notices and helps him, but reminds him to always ask for help.

Leo enjoys the zoo

Leo and his best friend Mia have a great time. He even asks a question to the zookeeper about monkey friendships!
